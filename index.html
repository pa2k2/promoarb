How a combo works

Three moves. The order matters — that's the whole trick.

01

Take the protected side

Open the loss-protection book (say BetMGM's $1,500 first-bet offer) and bet one side of a game. If it wins, you're paid in cash. If it loses, the stake comes back as bonus bets.

02

Hedge on the guaranteed side

On a second book with a guaranteed offer (like bet-and-get bonus bets), take the opposite side of the same game. Its bonus pays out regardless of the result.

03

Convert and collect

One side of your hedge always wins in cash, and the bonus bets convert to roughly 70¢ on the dollar by hedging them the same way. Either branch of the slip lands on the same number.
